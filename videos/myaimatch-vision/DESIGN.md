# myAImatch Vision Video Design

## Style Prompt

A cinematic investor explainer for myAImatch: dark-first, purple-led, premium, urgent, and strategic. The video should feel like a product thesis becoming inevitable: glass interfaces, local workflow signals, live catalog data, and a central AI workflow brain forming from brand light. Use restraint, high contrast, and precise motion instead of hype graphics.

## Colors

- Background: `#000000`
- Deep surface: `#111111`
- Primary purple: `#8468EB`
- Accent lavender: `#C4B5FD`
- Deep purple: `#5B42C3`
- Text: `#FFFFFF`
- Muted text: `rgba(255,255,255,0.66)`
- Dim text: `rgba(255,255,255,0.38)`

## Typography

- Primary: `Inter`, system sans-serif
- Weight range: 400 body, 700 captions, 800-900 cinematic statements
- Use large, readable type for video: 64px+ for scene statements, 34px+ for captions, 20px+ for labels.

## Motion

- Premium cinematic pace: build, breathe, transition.
- Use focus-pull blur crossfades, cinematic zooms, and color dips to black.
- Scene entrances must vary by element: y, x, scale, opacity, line reveal, and glow reveal.
- Data trails move continuously but deterministically.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not introduce any hue outside the purple family.
- Do not use generic blue SaaS gradients.
- Do not use jump cuts between scenes.
- Do not use rounded stock-like cards as the dominant visual.
- Do not make captions long subtitle blocks.
